About

This application displays UK Orienteering fixtures.

Fixtures are displayed from:
  • British Orienteering fixtures
  • British Army Orienteering Association fixtures

The user may logon to allow them to 'like' fixtures. In future there is an intention to allow for the reservation of maps

The fixture list may be filtered by:
  • Day of week (Sat/Sun/Weekday)
  • 'Liked' fixtures
  • Distance from home postcode for various grades of event.
